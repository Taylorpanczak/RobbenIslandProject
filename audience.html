Audience

The audience will be late primary school students and educators in South Africa and mid-Michigan, because it will serve as an introductory program into international civil rights and history. It will build a community as people from varying sociocultural experiences can communicate around a central topic real time. After the project is established, it will expand into other parts of the United States and the English-speaking world. However, the project will be publicly available from the beginning for any educators who choose to use it.
